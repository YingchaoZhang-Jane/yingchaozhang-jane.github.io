Yingchao Zhang, PhD

Visiting Professor, University of Cambridge
Assistant Professor (Tenured), Durham University

I am an economist specialising in labour and personnel economics, entrepreneurship, and firm performance, with a focus on SMEs, ESG, and innovation. My research integrates applied microeconomics and experimental methods to study how firms grow, adapt, and respond to technological and sustainability challenges.

I am Investigator of a major research funding project on SME growth and regional development (£752,474). My work has been published in journals including Small Business Economics, with ongoing projects targeting leading outlets such as Management Science, Research Policy, and the Human Resource Management Journal etc.
